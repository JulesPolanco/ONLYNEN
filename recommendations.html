Anime Recommendations

Haikyu: Where do I start? haikyu is such amazing series. It has all the right elements in terms of a sports series. I never thought I find myself saying a volleyball series would be my number 1 anime but it is. The vast and large of cast of character to the world bulidng everything is amazing. It currently not airing with a total of 4 seasons 79 episodes. You can Watch Haikyu on Cruchyroll.
Code Geass: Code Geass is one of the holy trinity of anime. It is regarded as master piece even though the series is 16 years old. I love this series and it is must watch anime espically for new comers.The Mecha/Sci-Fi theme is amazing and works hand and hand with the political elements of the series. Watch Code Geass on Netflix, Hulu, or Crunchyroll.The anime has 50 episodes and is completed.
Black Clover is my stuff, Black Clover is about a world of magic where the main character Asta is magicless but vows to be Wizard King, which is the highest ranking a mage can obtain, He ends up joining a very diverse and unique magic knight squad Black Bulls. Read Black Clover on Viz Media and Manga Plus.
Demon Slayer: Demon Slayer is insane if you like over the top animation, great cast of characters, good story, and amazing fight this is the series for you. This series is really great for new anime viewrs and easy to follow. Watch Demon Slayer on Netflix, Hulu, or Crunchyroll anime currently has 2 seasons with 35 epsiodes and a flim. Season 3 has been announced!
My Hero Academia is a very known series but I wanted to bring it to the page because it is such an easy series to get into for a manga if you like Marvel and DC Comics this series is for you. My Hero Academia is about a boy name Izuku Midoriya who is born quirkless but has an encounter with the number 1 hero All Might who deems Izuku worthy of obtaining his power. Read My Hero Academia on Viz Media and Manga Plus.
